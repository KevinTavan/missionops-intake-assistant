# Responsible AI Notes

This prototype is designed around human review, source visibility, and clear boundaries.

## AI should help with

- Summarizing source notes
- Extracting need categories
- Flagging missing information
- Preparing draft next steps
- Creating consistent handoff documentation

## AI should not do

- Final eligibility decisions
- Legal advice
- Medical advice
- Crisis counseling
- Housing determinations
- Benefit approvals or denials
- Sensitive prioritization without human review

## Design principles

### 1. Human review by default
Every output is a draft.

### 2. Missing information should stay missing
The system should say what is not specified instead of filling gaps.

### 3. Source evidence matters
Structured outputs should point back to source notes whenever possible.

### 4. Sensitive cases need escalation
Urgency flags are not final decisions. They are prompts for trained staff review.

### 5. Local policy wins
Each organization should adapt categories, risk thresholds, and next-step language to its own policies.
