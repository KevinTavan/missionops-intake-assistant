# Problem Statement

Mission-driven organizations often handle information through unstructured notes: intake forms, call logs, case updates, emails, and staff meeting notes. These notes contain important context, but staff may not have time to turn every note into a clean summary or next-step plan.

## Core problem

Important client context can be difficult to review quickly when notes are long, inconsistent, or scattered across systems.

## Example risks

- Missing urgent deadlines
- Repeating questions the client has already answered
- Losing context during staff handoff
- Confusing program needs across housing, food, employment, education, transportation, or legal support
- Over-relying on AI summaries without source review

## Proposed workflow

The MissionOps Intake Assistant creates a structured draft brief from source notes. The staff member reviews the draft, confirms missing information, and makes final decisions using organizational policy.

## Success criteria

A useful workflow should:

1. Save staff review time
2. Preserve source context
3. Identify missing information
4. Avoid unsupported claims
5. Escalate urgent risk indicators for human review
6. Be easy for another team member to understand and maintain
