# Evaluation Rubric

Use this rubric to review AI-generated intake briefs.

| Category | 1 | 3 | 5 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accuracy | Major unsupported claims | Mostly accurate with small issues | Fully supported by source |
| Completeness | Misses key need or deadline | Captures main need but misses nuance | Captures key needs, deadlines, and constraints |
| Missing info handling | Guesses unknowns | Some unknowns marked | Unknowns clearly marked and converted into follow-up questions |
| Urgency handling | Misses urgent signal | Flags urgency vaguely | Clearly flags urgency and routes to human review |
| Tone | Overconfident or inappropriate | Mostly professional | Clear, careful, and staff-ready |
| Handoff readiness | Hard to use | Usable with edits | Ready for human review and documentation |

## Suggested review process

1. Compare summary against source note.
2. Circle any unsupported claim.
3. Check whether missing details are labeled.
4. Check whether next steps are appropriate for staff role.
5. Confirm no legal, medical, or eligibility decision is made by AI.
